SULPHO OAVS

OAVS, ortho anisidine vinyl sulphone is a very important intermediate, OAVS, ortho anisidine vinyl sulphone is used with other intermediate to manufacture quality products.

Applications & Uses :

1. Dye Manufacturing: Sulpho OAVS is used as an intermediate in the synthesis of azo dyes and pigments. It contributes to the production of vibrant and stable colors used in textiles, plastics, and other materials.
2. Textile Industry: It finds applications in the dyeing and printing of textiles, such as cotton, wool, and silk. Sulpho OAVS imparts color to fabrics and enhances their appearance and durability.
3. Paper Industry: Sulpho OAVS is utilized in the production of colored paper and paper products, including stationery, packaging materials, and decorative paper.
4. Inks: It is used in the formulation of printing inks for paper and fabric, as well as inks for writing instruments. Sulpho OAVS contributes to the coloration and quality of printed materials.
5. Leather Industry: Sulpho OAVS finds applications in the dyeing and finishing of leather goods, such as shoes, handbags, and belts. It enhances the color and appearance of leather products.
6. Research: Sulpho OAVS may also be used in scientific research for various purposes, including as a reagent in organic synthesis and as a reference compound in spectroscopic analysis.

Storage Notes :

1. Container: Store Sulpho VS in a tightly sealed container made of a compatible material, such as glass or polyethylene, to prevent contamination and exposure to air or moisture.
2. Environment: Keep the storage area cool, dry, and well-ventilated. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight, heat sources, and ignition sparks.
3. Chemical Compatibility: Store Sulpho OAVS away from incompatible substances or reactive chemicals to prevent adverse reactions.
4. Handling Precautions:When handling Sulpho OAVS, use appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) such as gloves, goggles, and a lab coat to avoid skin contact, eye irritation, or inhalation of vapors.
5. Labeling: Clearly label the storage container with the chemical name, hazards, handling precautions, and any other relevant information for safe storage and handling.
6. First Aid: Have a designated first aid kit and procedures in place in case of accidental exposure or spills. Train personnel on proper handling and emergency response protocols.
7. Security: Store Sulpho OAVS in a secure location to prevent unauthorized access or tampering, especially in facilities where security is a concern.
